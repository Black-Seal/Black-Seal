# Greetings! 👋


I am currently an undergraduate student at the Singapore Institute of Technology, pursuing a Bachelor of Science degree in Applied Artificial Intelligence.

I earned my Diploma in Biomedical Science from Ngee Ann Polytechnic in 2021. My journey into the world of technology began with my discovery of Python, which quickly became my first programming language. During my time in polytechnic, I dedicated my holidays to enrolling in programming and data science courses. It was during this period that I developed a deep passion for technology. This newfound passion ultimately led me to make the bold decision to transition into the tech industry.

I am also currently a student at 42 Singapore, a coding school situated at the Singapore University of Technology and Design. At this institution, I have acquired a profound understanding of C programming and bash scripting. What sets this school apart is its distinctive approach: there are no traditional teachers, and all students are encouraged to learn from one another.
